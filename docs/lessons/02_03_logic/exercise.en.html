📋 Task

Let's create a program to find who can ride the attraction among 4 people at a theme park.

The attraction requirements are: "6 years or older" AND "120cm or taller".

Data for 4 People

  • A: 8 years old, 125cm
  • B: 5 years old, 130cm
  • C: 10 years old, 115cm
  • D: 4 years old, 140cm

Print the name (A, B, C, or D) of the person who meets the requirements.

✏️ Instructions

  • The data (age and height) for all 4 people are already defined as variables
  • Use the and operator to check both age and height requirements
  • For each person, use an if statement to check the conditions, and print() the name of those who meet them
  • We won't use functions (haven't learned them yet)
  • We won't use loops either (haven't learned them yet)
  • Write condition checks for all 4 people
