📋 Task

Write a program that assigns your age to a variable, calculates your age in 10 years, and displays it.

For example, if you're 14 now, you'll be 24 in 10 years.

✏️ Instructions

  • Assign your age to variable age
  • Assign age + 10 to variable future_age
  • Display future_age using print()

▶ 💡 Hints

  • First, assign your current age like age = 14
  • Next, calculate 10 years later with future_age = age + 10
  • Finally, display the result with print(future_age)
